FAQs

You Have Questions,

We Have Answers

A. Loan Products

What types of loan products do you offer?

We offer four loan products: Educredit, Youthcredit, Quickcredit, and Ecocredit. Each product is designed to meet the specific needs of tertiary students, young entrepreneurs, low-income individuals, and small business owners.

How do I apply for a loan?

You can apply for a loan by filling out our online application form or visiting our office.

What are the eligibility criteria for your loan products?

Eligibility criteria vary by product but generally include age, identification, income, credit history, and capacity to pay.

How much can I borrow?

Loan amounts vary by product and individual circumstances, but we offer flexible loan options to suit your needs. Please contact us for more information.

What is the interest rate on your loan products?

Our interest rates are competitive and vary depending on the loan product. Please contact us for more information.

B. Application and Products

How long does it take to process a loan application?

Our approval process is designed to be quick and efficient, with decisions typically made within 2-7 hours.

What documents do I need to provide to apply for a loan?

You'll need to provide identification, proof of income, and other supporting documents. Please contact us for more information.

How will I know if my loan application is approved?

We'll notify you via email or phone once your loan application is approved.

Can I apply for a loan if I have a poor credit history?

No, we do not consider loan applications from customers with poor credit histories.

C. Repayment

What are the repayment terms for your loans?

Repayment terms vary by product, but we offer flexible repayment options to suit your needs.

How do I repay my loan?

You can repay your loan through our mobile money short code or by visiting our office.

What happens if I miss a repayment?

We’re customer centric and will discuss with you to understand your reasons for default and offer you grace periods or revised terms. Late payment fees may apply.

What are the consequences of late loan repayment?

Late loan repayment may attract penalties and affect your credit score. Please contact us if you're experiencing difficulties repaying your loan.

Can I repay my loan early?

Yes, you can repay your loan early without any penalties. Early repayment can help you save on interest costs.

D. General Questions

Is your financial service regulated?

Yes, we are regulated by relevant financial authorities, including the Microcredit Association of Ghana (MCAG) and the Bank of Ghana (BoG).

How do you protect my personal and financial data?

We take data protection seriously and implement robust security measures to safeguard your information. Your information is stored securely and only accessible to authorized personnel.

How do I contact JASHFIN Enterprise?

You can contact us through our website, email, phone, or social media.

What are your office hours?

Our office hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on weekends and public holidays.
